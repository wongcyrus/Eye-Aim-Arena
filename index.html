♿ Rehabilitation & Accessibility

Reduces the number of targets during calibration. 3-point calibration is highly recommended for children who get tired easily or have highly restricted head/eye movements.

Strongly dampens involuntary head tremors or eye jitter, making targeting easier for paralyzed children.

Shrinks the random target generation area. Restricting targets to the screen center accommodates limited neck mobility and avoids strain.
