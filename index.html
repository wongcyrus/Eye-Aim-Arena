📋 Setup Checklist

Please check the following before calibrating for best results:

  • 👁️ Eye level: Keep your eyes approximately horizontal with the camera lens
  • 📏 Distance: Sit 40–70 cm (16–28 inches) from the camera
  • 🎯 Face position: Center your face within the camera view
  • 💡 Lighting: Use even, front-facing light; avoid strong backlights or shadows
  • 🧘 Head stability: Keep your head as still as possible during calibration
